We extend our deepest gratitude to Hubballi-Dharwad Municipal Corporation (HDMC), Revolution Minds, and Hasirudala for their generous support, kindness, and unwavering commitment towards helping underprivileged communities. Their collaboration and dedication are the backbone of our mission to bring dignity and hope through cloth donation. Together, we are making a meaningful¬†difference.

HDMC

HDMC is helping us by providing access to public spaces to set up donation centres across the city. This allows easy access for donors and helps expand our reach. Role: Donation centre support and public space access Location: Hubballi-Dharwad, Karnataka

Revolutionary Minds

Revolution Minds is a youth-led platform focused on driving social change through innovation and collaboration. They are helping us with awareness, technology, and donor engagement. Role: Awareness building, donor engagement, and digital support Location: Hubli, Karnataka

Hasirudala

Hasirudala is a social impact organization that works with waste pickers to create inclusive waste management systems. They are supporting us with cloth collection, sorting, and eco-friendly disposal. Role: Cloth collection, sorting, and sustainable reuse Location: Hubli,¬†Karnataka
